My Publications

Below is the ongoing research project that I have been working on. While I have only a limited number of publications so far, I am committed to pursuing excellence and expanding my scholarly contributions further.

A Conceptual Model of Trust in Generative AI Systems

I had the privilege of collaborating with Dr. Elham Rastegari and Dr. Nargess Tahmasbi in this research project which examines the factors that influence user trust in GAI. Our work emphasizes the critical role of HCI research in developing ethical, and user-centered technologies. The paper has been accepted for publication at the Hawaii International Conference on System Sciences (HICSS) but the project is still in progress of collecting data. The final work will be updated on this page once the paper is published. Please click here to view our draft.

Creighton Analytical and Institutional Research Internship

As a Data Analyst Intern under the supervision of Dr. Kristin Buscher at Creighton University, I gained hands-on experience working with institutional data to support university planning and strategy. This internship helped me grow both technically and professionally, strengthening my skills in data analysis, visualization, communication, and independent problem-solving.

Creighton Data Dashboard

Built an end-to-end Tableau dashboard by cleaning and transforming raw institutional data. Designed for the Provost’s office and stakeholders, it helps visualize key university metrics to support internal decision-making.

Creighton Ranking Simulator

Developed an interactive application to project Creighton’s 2026 rank. Users can adjust values and instantly see how the changes impact ranking. The interface also visualizes key metrics in bar graphs and provides insights to support data-driven decisions.

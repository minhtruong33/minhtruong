Basic Financial Tracker

This financial tracker was created for a business proposal in the BlueBox Project. It aims to help non-profit businesses better track their transactions and interpret their business performance. The design was proposed by a BlueBox student in my group and I was responsible for the idea and the design of both front end and backend of this.
